Everything you need to know.

pquote is a field quoting tool built for contractors and trade crews. It lets you measure areas using satellite maps, set prices per service type, and send professional quotes via text, email, or PDF — all from your phone in under 60 seconds.

Any device with a web browser — iPhone, Android, iPad, laptop, desktop. It's a Progressive Web App (PWA), so you can install it to your home screen for an app-like experience. No app store needed.

Yes — pquote is in early access beta. Every feature is free while we're building and refining the product with real users. When we introduce pricing, early users will get the best deal.

Pressure washing, parking lot striping, sealcoating, painting, roofing, concrete, landscaping, and a custom/other option. Each service type has its own quick-price buttons and remembers your last-used rate.

Tap "Open Map & Draw" on the measurement step. You'll see a full-screen satellite view centered on the job address. Tap the corners of the work area to draw a polygon. pquote calculates the area using real-world geometry (haversine + shoelace formula) and converts it to sq ft, sq yd, and acres automatically.

All connections use HTTPS encryption. Your quotes are stored on our servers and password-protected. Client names, prices, and tax rates are cached locally on your device for speed — never shared with third parties.

Yes. On the review step, tap "Add another service" to add as many line items as you need. Each can have a different service type, area, unit, and price. The total calculates automatically with optional tax.
